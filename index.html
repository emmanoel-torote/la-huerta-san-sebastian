A Living Canvas of Mexican Architecture

Imagine walking through an orchard of mature trees, discovering your own microclimate among the ancient cardons and native flora. This is La Huerta San Sebastián.

A curated collection of 10 unique homes, each individually designed by one of five renowned Mexican architects. Created for those who value harmony between nature, architecture, and coastal living, these residences blend contemporary design with the natural warmth of the Baja landscape.

Inspired by Mexican aesthetics, each home offers a seamless indoor-outdoor experience, reflecting the artistic spirit and laid-back rhythm of Todos Santos. On 2,000 m² lots that honor the original orchard landscape, these architectural statements are more than homes—they are a way of life.
